Favorite movie

My favorite movie is Nerve because I think it accurately represents our generation and how dependent we are on technology. The game Nerve is addicting; People accept reckless dares, like reaching 60 MPH on a motorcycle (blindfolded) for money. Nerve is a technology-obsessed movie.

Favorite actress

I think she can adjust to roles really well since her character in Nerve and her character in Scream Queens are almost two characters with opposite personalities.
